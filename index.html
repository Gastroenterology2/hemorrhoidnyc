Hemorrhoid Types Treatment Nyc

Internal Hemorrhoids

These occur inside the rectum. They usually cause painless rectal bleeding or blood from the anus.

External Hemorrhoids

Found under the skin around the anus, these can be painful, swollen, and irritated.

Thrombosed Hemorrhoids

External hemorrhoids that develop a blood clot, causing severe pain, swelling, and a hard lump.

Prolapsed Hemorrhoids

Internal hemorrhoids that bulge out of the anus, often causing discomfort and bleeding.
